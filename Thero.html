About

Bagawanthalawe Rahula Thero is a Buddhist monk who resides in Sri Lanka, specifically in the temple of Mandawala located in the city of Balangoda. He was ordained in the year 2015 and has since dedicated his life to serving the community. One of the most notable things about Bagawanthalawe Rahula Thero is his passion for community service. He strongly believes in the power of helping others and making a positive impact on the world. His dedication to community service has inspired many, and he continues to make a difference in the lives of those around him. Bagawanthalawe Rahula Thero's work in the community is a true reflection of his commitment to Buddhism. He upholds the Buddhist values of compassion, generosity, and kindness, and uses these values to guide his actions in serving the community.
